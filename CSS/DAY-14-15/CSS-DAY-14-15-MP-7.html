Tasty Dish Name

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Stir well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
